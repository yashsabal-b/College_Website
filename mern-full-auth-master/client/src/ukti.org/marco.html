Marcographia

The Photography Club

The photography club of Jain JC, Marcographia, showcases the talents of many students who have indulged themselves in the art and craft of photography. Take a look through the lens of our camera and see the world and life as it is and capture the essence of it and bask in its awe.Our team believes that each and every shot is special, memories just waiting to be captured and etched in one photo as though it were art. Our team believes in capturing moments and recording memories to cherish forever. There is detail in every shot, there is poise in every expression, and utmost respect to those who lend us their time to help make their days and lives memorable. We do our work with passion and always maintain a positive mentality in everything we do and hoping we can freeze time and keep everything still and lost in time for you.

The cultural forum of Jain Deemed-to-be-University aims at providing a diverse cultural work place for students to showcase their cultural prowess. It is a platform that houses a melange of talented individuals who are the artful masters of their crafts. In order to ensure the holistic development of every student UKTI hosts a plethora of events and fests, which promotes a sense of kinship and sportsman ship among the students of various streams within the campus and various universities.
