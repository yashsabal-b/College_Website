Arohi

Jain university - School of Sciences has a great team of musicians and music enthusiasts. This club includes a diverse grgoup of musicians from various genres of music; be it the austerity of Indian classical music, the varied colours of Indian pop, serene melodies and beats of Western acoustics.


The Western Team

With, a perfect blend of harmonies, acoustics and a capella, this group of amazing individuals come up with a soothing potion of melodies that will take your music pores off to an euphonic experience.


The Indian Team

Samvaad

The Indian music team, brings a banging combination of different regional languages, melodies and mostly, feelings. Because the feelings are what bring us all together as indians and musicians.


Classical Team

As for the Indian bards of the yore, they indulge you in a world of laya, raaga and rasa .The texture and ambience created by us, will take you to the ancient world but still leave you gnawing for more. The Indian Classical team brings the vibes of Raagas of different genres of Indian Classics- Be it Carnatic or Hindustani or a Fusion of both. Each genre is a melody to the heart.

The cultural forum of Jain Deemed-to-be-University aims at providing a diverse cultural work place for students to showcase their cultural prowess. It is a platform that houses a melange of talented individuals who are the artful masters of their crafts. In order to ensure the holistic development of every student UKTI hosts a plethora of events and fests, which promotes a sense of kinship and sportsman ship among the students of various streams within the campus and various universities.
