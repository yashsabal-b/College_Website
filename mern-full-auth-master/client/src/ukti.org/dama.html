Damaruga

The Theatre Team

Learning the art of drama was our dream So we stand here as Damaruga -The Theater Team. Jain University- School of Sciences has brought together amazing talents who believe in creating a difference via different forms of theater. Mime, street play, short films, you name it, and we are always ready to rock your world with our best of performances. Just like our name suggests, we believe in sireing energy and making the world a better place with a touch of entertainment. Damaruga started as a club in the year 2018 by the now Theater Head M.P.Dechamma. Our club was supported by our beloved Director Dr. Asha Rajeev, Miss. Jharna Mohanti, Miss Pallavi Prahlad and many other faculty members along with our senior Athul Krishnan. We started off as a mime team and won a lot of hearts. We then brought in performances which included drama and street play and found success there as well. Following which we thought of creating short films, which for our surprise ended up being loved by many people. Our journey was not too smooth but as the saying goes life is a road with ups and downs and with a lot of hurdles but the main thing to focus on is that we stood through those hurdles and have reached a position which represents unity and strength.

The cultural forum of Jain Deemed-to-be-University aims at providing a diverse cultural work place for students to showcase their cultural prowess. It is a platform that houses a melange of talented individuals who are the artful masters of their crafts. In order to ensure the holistic development of every student UKTI hosts a plethora of events and fests, which promotes a sense of kinship and sportsman ship among the students of various streams within the campus and various universities.
