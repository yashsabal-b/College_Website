The Fashion Team

Hola,
We introduce you to the Jain JC Fashion Team, Tarashna The heads Sakshi Hemant and Anthea M Rayan are all about celebrating person's creativity and aura; on and off stage. Fashion is not all about sketching and the glitz of the catwalk; there is hard work and the desire to develop a solid foundation along with learning new skills and qualities.

The team aims at syncing extrinsic with intrinsic fashion as a purpose to feel alive and present. The main objective of Tarashna is to decorate human and their life with colors, design and patterns that can accentuate the eyes.

Our world is very volatile. It keeps on changing due to various influential factors. This has made a great aura of creativity and imagination that is creating new trends globally and we therefore start to individualize ourselves while looking at which social group we belong. Fashion is real and raw; securing a feeling to nurture. It's a way of celebrating diversity and variety of the world in which we live in. It's never about a single person, we craft identity as one team. Everyone likes to have their own fashion statement , and Tarashna let's you have it.

The cultural forum of Jain Deemed-to-be-University aims at providing a diverse cultural work place for students to showcase their cultural prowess. It is a platform that houses a melange of talented individuals who are the artful masters of their crafts. In order to ensure the holistic development of every student UKTI hosts a plethora of events and fests, which promotes a sense of kinship and sportsman ship among the students of various streams within the campus and various universities.
